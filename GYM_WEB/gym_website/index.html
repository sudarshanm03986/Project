About

Heya! My name is Andre Oreta, I am currently in my early 20's and I compete as a U93kg Junior. Currently, I am being coached and mentored by James Schroeder who is a well known powerlifting coach in New Zealand. James has taught me much in strength training and I am hoping to use these skills on both novice and veteran lifters alike.

My educational background consists of a Certification in Personal Training and I am currently enrolled in the Bachelor of Sports and Exercise Science. My passion exists in the strength sports and more specifically, in powerlifting and overall strength development. I love seeing people gain strength and seeing the same passion spark in their eyes. My total time spent in the gym is nearing 5 years with my current total sitting comfortably at 700kg.

My personal ambitions as a lifter extends out farther and I hope to make a name for myself at IPF Worlds and be competitive in my current weight class. As a coach, my ambition is to grow and develop as many lifters and having them be as strong as they possibly can and still break that limit further with them.

Outside of powerlifting, my interest lies in MMA, American Football, League, and Rugby Union. During my downtime, I usually like to be kept up to date on new knowledge in the fitness industry. I believe the more up to date I am, the more I can give to my clients.
